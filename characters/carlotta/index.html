💡 Why This Build Works

Why Last Dance: As a ★★★★★ Pistols, Last Dance is the optimal choice for Carlotta. CRIT DMG is the highest-value DPS stat, multiplying all damage output. Its passive "Finale Waltz" — increases resonance liberation dmg by 30%. after casting resonance liberation, gains 10% crit rate for 10s. — synergizes directly with Carlotta's playstyle.

Why Lampylumen Myriad: As a Cost 4 Glacio Echo, Lampylumen Myriad provides the best active skill for Carlotta. The Freezing Frost Sonata set (Glacio DMG +10% (2pc), +30% after Basic Attack (5pc)) directly amplifies Carlotta's Glacio damage output. Its skill "Myriad Ice" — summon multiple ice crystals that explode after a delay, dealing glacio dmg. each crystal that hits reduces the cooldown... — adds valuable combat utility.

Why this team: Shorekeeper (support): Universal CRIT buff · Sustain. Verina (healer): ATK buff · Healing for ranged DPS. Zhezhi (subdps): Glacio Resonance · Res. Skill DMG buff.
