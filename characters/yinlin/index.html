Yinlin

★★★★★ ⚡ Electro 📿 Rectifier Natural

The enigmatic consultant of the Public Security Bureau, Yinlin manipulates Electro energy with surgical precision. Her rectifier generates strings of lightning that mark and detonate targets from afar. Calm, calculating, and devastatingly effective — she never misses her mark.

Basic Attack
Basic Attack — Arc Lash
Perform up to 4 ranged Electro attacks with the rectifier. The 4th attack fires a spread of 3 projectiles.
Resonance Skill
Resonance Skill — Zapstring Mark
⏱ CD 8s.⚡ Energy 8.
Fire a lightning bolt at the target location, dealing Electro DMG and applying Zapstring mark. Marked enemies take bonus Electro DMG and explode after 3s for additional AoE DMG. CD: 8s. Concerto Energy: 8.
Resonance Liberation
Resonance Liberation — Thunderstorm Decree
⏱ CD 20s.⚡ Energy 125.
Call down a devastating thunderstorm that strikes all enemies in a wide area, dealing massive Electro DMG. All active Zapstring marks detonate instantly for double damage. CD: 20s. Energy Cost: 125.
Forte Circuit
Forte Circuit — Conductive Web
When Zapstring marks detonate, they chain lightning to nearby unmarked enemies (max 3 chains). Chain lightning applies a weaker Zapstring. Each detonation grants 3 Concerto Energy.
Outro Skill
Outro Skill — Static Discharge
Deal Electro DMG equal to 250% of ATK. Increase the incoming Resonator's Electro DMG by 23% and Resonance Liberation DMG by 20% for 14s.

💡 Why This Build Works

Why Stringmaster: As a ★★★★★ Rectifier, Stringmaster is the optimal choice for Yinlin. CRIT Rate is the highest-value DPS stat, multiplying all damage output. Its passive "Resonance Quartet" — when resonance skill hits a target, increases atk by 3% for 8s, stacking up to 5 times. each stack is independent. — synergizes directly with Yinlin's playstyle.

Why Thundering Mephis: As a Cost 4 Electro Echo, Thundering Mephis provides the best active skill for Yinlin. The Void Thunder Sonata set (Electro DMG +10% (2pc), +30% after Heavy Attack (5pc)) directly amplifies Yinlin's Electro damage output. Its skill "Thunderous Dive" — transform into thundering mephis and dive at enemies, dealing massive electro dmg. upon landing, create a lightning fiel... — adds valuable combat utility.

Why this team: Shorekeeper (support): Universal CRIT buff · Sustain. Verina (healer): ATK buff · Team-wide healing. Jinhsi (dps): Electro buffs Jinhsi's Res. Skill · Off-field coverage.
