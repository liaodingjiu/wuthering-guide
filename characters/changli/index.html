Changli

★★★★★ 🔥 Fusion 🗡️ Sword Natural

A blazing swordswoman whose passion burns as bright as her flames. Changli wields Fusion energy with the grace of a dancer and the ferocity of a wildfire, each strike leaving trails of fire that consume everything in their path. Her motto: fight first, ask questions never.

⚔️ Skills

Basic Attack
Basic Attack — Blazing Dance
Perform up to 5 elegant sword strikes, dealing Fusion DMG. The 4th and 5th strikes deal increased DMG and apply a burn effect. Dodge Counter: a swift flame slash.
Resonance Skill
Resonance Skill — Phoenix Dash
⚡ Energy 10.
Perform a fiery dash-slash through enemies, dealing Fusion DMG and applying Ignite (DoT). Holds 2 charges. Can be used mid-air. CD per charge: 8s. Concerto Energy: 10.
Resonance Liberation
Resonance Liberation — Inferno Phoenix
⏱ CD 22s.⚡ Energy 125.
Summon a blazing phoenix that sweeps across the battlefield, dealing massive AoE Fusion DMG and refreshing Resonance Skill charges. CD: 22s. Energy Cost: 125.
Forte Circuit
Forte Circuit — Blaze Accumulation
Dealing Fusion DMG accumulates Blaze stacks (max 8). At max stacks, the next Resonance Skill deals 60% increased DMG and applies a stronger burn effect that lasts 10s.
Outro Skill
Outro Skill — Ember's Gift
Deal Fusion DMG equal to 260% of ATK. Increase the incoming Resonator's Fusion DMG by 23% and Resonance Skill DMG by 20% for 14s.
