💡 Why This Build Works

Why Blazing Brilliance: As a ★★★★★ Sword, Blazing Brilliance is the optimal choice for Yangyang. CRIT DMG is the highest-value DPS stat, multiplying all damage output. Its passive "Scorching Precipice" — increases resonance skill dmg by 24%. after casting resonance skill, basic attack dmg increases by 30% for 10s. — synergizes directly with Yangyang's playstyle.

Why Dragon of Dirge: As a Cost 4 Aero Echo, Dragon of Dirge provides the best active skill for Yangyang. The Sierra Gale Sonata set (Aero DMG +10% (2pc), +30% after Intro Skill (5pc)) directly amplifies Yangyang's Aero damage output. Its skill "Dirge Breath" — transform into dragon of dirge and breathe a torrent of wind, dealing aero dmg in a large cone.... — adds valuable combat utility.

Why this team: Verina (healer): Universal ATK buff · Team-wide healing. Shorekeeper (support): Universal CRIT buff · Sustain.
