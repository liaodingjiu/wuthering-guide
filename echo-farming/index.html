Echo Farming Guide

Stop wasting Waveplates. Master the +5 stop-loss method, optimize your weekly boss rotation, and build god-tier echo sets with maximum efficiency.

🔧 The +5 Stop-Loss Method (Save 70% of Your EXP)

The biggest mistake players make is leveling an echo all the way to +25 only to discover the substats are garbage. This is how you stop doing that.

🧠 The Concept: Echo substats unlock at levels 5, 10, 15, 20, and 25. You CANNOT reroll individual substats. If your first substat is bad, the echo is almost certainly trash. Cut your losses at +5.
1

Level to +5 — Check the first substat

Use the cheapest echo EXP material for this. If the first substat is CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, ATK%, or Energy Regen → continue. If it's flat DEF, flat HP, or HP% → stop and recycle.

2

Level to +10 — Check the second substat

If you've hit at least one strong substat, push to +10. Two good substats by +10 = keep going. One good + one bad = consider recycling depending on how rare the echo is.

3

Level to +15 — The decision point

At +15 you've revealed 3 substats. Keep if: 2+ strong lines. Recycle if: only 1 or 0 strong lines. Cost 4 boss echoes get more leniency — they're harder to farm.

4

Level to +20 and +25 — Only if it's already good

Only push to max if the echo has 3+ desirable substats by +15. A 3-good-stat echo at +25 is better than a 5-bad-stat echo — don't sink EXP into mediocrity.

📊 By the numbers: Following this method, you recycle roughly 70% of echoes at +5, 20% at +10/+15, and only fully invest in the top 10%. This saves approximately 70% of your total echo EXP compared to blindly leveling everything to +25.

🗺️ Where to Farm Echoes

🌍 Open World (Free)

Defeat overworld enemies and absorb their echoes. Higher Data Bank = better drop rates (up to 20% base). Use F2 → monster icon to track specific echoes. Cost 4 boss echoes reset weekly — farm them every Monday.

⚡ Tacet Fields (Waveplates)

Spend Waveplates here for guaranteed echoes of a specific Sonata set. Also drops echo EXP and tuning materials. Always target the set your character needs. Never let Waveplates cap at 240.

🎯 Fantasies of a Thousand Gateways

Alternative Waveplate spending option. Some players report better echo drop rates here vs standard Tacet Fields. Worth testing once your characters are built.

🏆 Weekly Bosses (3/week)

Weekly bosses drop high-quality echoes and materials. Claim 3 per week. Priority: bosses that drop Cost 4 echoes matching your active team's sets.

📅 Recommended Weekly Farming Routine

  1. Monday — Weekly reset: Kill all 3 weekly bosses. Farm Cost 4 overworld boss echoes for your active team (free, no Waveplates).
  2. Daily — Spend Waveplates: Run the Tacet Field for your target Sonata set. If you don't have a specific target, farm the set for your Main DPS.
  3. Wednesday/Weekend — Tuning session: Go through your echo backlog. Level everything to +5, sort into keep/recycle piles. Push promising echoes to +10/+15.
  4. Before logging off: Check Waveplates. If near cap (240), condense into Crystal Solvents or spend them. Never waste regeneration.
