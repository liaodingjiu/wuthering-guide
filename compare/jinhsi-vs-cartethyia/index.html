Jinhsi vs Cartethyia

★★★★★SpectroBroadblade VS ★★★★★AeroSword

Jinhsi remains the biggest single-target nuke in the game, while Cartethyia has emerged as the AoE queen with her Aero Erosion mechanics. Both are top-tier, but they play very differently and fit different account needs.

📊 Side-by-Side Comparison

Jinhsi

Still the biggest nuke in Wuthering Waves as of 2026. Incandescence stacks enable massive single-hit damage that can one-shot elite enemies. Excels in Tower of Adversity boss floors.

Cartethyia

Best AoE DPS in the game. Erosion damage ticks continuously, making her the Whimpering Wastes queen. Lower single-target peak but higher total damage in multi-enemy scenarios.

Jinhsi

Needs Spectro supports for resonance. Works well with Yinlin (off-field Electro + Res Skill buff) and universal healers. Pairs with Zhezhi for coordinated attacks.

Cartethyia

Wants Ciaccona for optimal Aero synergy. Team building is more restrictive but the payoff in AoE content is massive. Aero resonance provides energy generation benefits.

Jinhsi

Aged like fine wine — still competitive two years after release. If you already have Jinhsi built, she remains a top-tier investment with no signs of falling off.

Cartethyia

Newer character with potentially longer shelf life ahead. If starting fresh, Cartethyia is the stronger pick for modern content (Whimpering Wastes focus).
