Shorekeeper vs Verina

★★★★★SpectroRectifier VS ★★★★★SpectroRectifier

Shorekeeper and Verina are the two best supports in Wuthering Waves. Both provide healing and team-wide buffs, but they excel in different areas. This comparison helps you decide which to prioritize for your account.

📊 Side-by-Side Comparison

Shorekeeper

Crit Rate + Crit DMG buffs for the entire team. Higher damage ceiling for any DPS. Also provides DMG amplification and a one-time KO revive.

Verina

Universal ATK% buff that scales with her own ATK. Consistent and reliable, but lower ceiling than Shorekeeper's crit buffs. Team-wide healing on every Outro.

Shorekeeper

Larger burst heals that can save a team from near-death. Synergizes with the 5pc Rejuvenating Glow set for maximum healing.

Verina

Consistent sustained healing. Less burst but more reliable over long fights. Easier to maintain full team HP without timing windows.

Shorekeeper

Slightly longer field time to build Concerto. The extra seconds are worth the damage amplification she provides on Outro.

Verina

Fastest Concerto generation in the game. Minimal field time means more DPS uptime. Perfect for quick-swap teams that want to cycle rapidly.

Shorekeeper

Fits 95% of all teams. Universal buffs work with any DPS element. The most future-proof support in the game as of 2026.

Verina

Also universal, but slightly less impactful buffs. Still top-tier and entirely free from the beginner selector. The best budget option for any account.

🏆 Verdict

Pick Shorekeeper if you want the highest possible damage ceiling and plan to invest long-term. Pick Verina if you're F2P, want the fastest rotations, or need a second healer for your other ToA team. Ideally build both — they're the two best supports in the game and every account needs at least two healers for endgame content.
