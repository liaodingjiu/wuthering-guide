Wuthering Waves Codes

Active redemption codes for free Astrite, Shell Credits, and resources. Copy with one click.

📋 How to Redeem

  1. Open Wuthering Waves and go to Settings (gear icon)
  2. Select Account → Redeem Code
  3. Enter the code and tap Confirm
  4. Rewards are sent to your in-game Mailbox
  5. Or redeem online at wutheringwaves.kurogames.com/en/gift

⚠️ Codes are case-sensitive and may expire without notice. Redeem as soon as possible.
