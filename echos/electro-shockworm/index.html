🗺️ How to Farm

🎯
Target: Tacet Field — Electro Region

Drops from Electro Tacet Discords. Cost 1 echoes have higher rates from boss-level enemies. Use Interactive Map → for spawn locations.

🔄
Merge & Upgrade Strategy

Feed unwanted lv.0 echoes to level promising ones to lv.5-10. If substats are bad, feed that echo to the next one — exp transfers efficiently.

💡
Substat Priority: CRIT > ATK% > Flat ATK

Use Tuners to reveal substats. Stop farming when you have CRIT Rate + CRIT DMG on Cost 4, and at least one CRIT stat on Cost 3/1 pieces. Read Echo Guide →

❓ Common Questions

Who should use Electro Shockworm?

Electro Shockworm is best for any Electro DPS character, providing the Void Thunder set bonus.

Where do I farm Electro Shockworm?

Drops from Electro Tacet Discords in the overworld. Cost 1 echoes also appear in Tacet Fields. Use the Interactive Map → for spawn locations.

Is Electro Shockworm worth farming?

Yes — as a Cost 1 piece, farm until you get CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG substats to complete your Void Thunder set.
