Snip Snap

Cost 1 ❄️ Glacio

A small but fierce echo with jaws that freeze on contact. Its bite is far worse than its bark, leaving patches of frost that linger for hours.

Sonata Effect

Active Skill

Frostbite
Bite enemies with frozen fangs, dealing Glacio DMG.

2-piece: Glacio DMG +10%.

🗺️ How to Farm

🎯
Target: Tacet Field — Glacio Region

Drops from Glacio Tacet Discords. Cost 1 echoes have higher rates from boss-level enemies. Use Interactive Map → for spawn locations.

🔄
Merge & Upgrade Strategy

Feed unwanted lv.0 echoes to level promising ones to lv.5-10. If substats are bad, feed that echo to the next one — exp transfers efficiently.

💡
Substat Priority: CRIT > ATK% > Flat ATK

Use Tuners to reveal substats. Stop farming when you have CRIT Rate + CRIT DMG on Cost 4, and at least one CRIT stat on Cost 3/1 pieces. Read Echo Guide →

❓ Common Questions

Who should use Snip Snap?

Snip Snap is best for any Glacio DPS character, providing the Freezing Frost set bonus.

Where do I farm Snip Snap?

Drops from Glacio Tacet Discords in the overworld. Cost 1 echoes also appear in Tacet Fields. Use the Interactive Map → for spawn locations.

Is Snip Snap worth farming?

Yes — as a Cost 1 piece, farm until you get CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG substats to complete your Freezing Frost set.
