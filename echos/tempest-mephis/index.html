Tempest Mephis

Cost 4 💨 Aero

A whirling echo born from the eye of an eternal cyclone. It dances on the wind with terrifying grace, leaving destruction in its spiral path.

💨 Aero
Element
💨 Sierra Gale
Sonata Effect

Active Skill

Cyclone Strike
Transform into Tempest Mephis and summon a cyclone that pulls enemies in, dealing Aero DMG. Increases Aero DMG Bonus by 12% for 15s after use.

Sonata Effect: 💨 Sierra Gale

2-piece: Aero DMG +10%.
5-piece: After casting Resonance Skill, Aero DMG +30% for 15s. After casting Intro Skill, Aero DMG +15% for 8s.

🗺️ How to Farm

🎯
Target: Tacet Field — Aero Region

Drops from Aero Tacet Discords. Cost 4 echoes have higher rates from boss-level enemies. Use Interactive Map → for spawn locations.

🔄
Merge & Upgrade Strategy

Feed unwanted lv.0 echoes to level promising ones to lv.5-10. If substats are bad, feed that echo to the next one — exp transfers efficiently.

💡
Substat Priority: CRIT > ATK% > Flat ATK

Use Tuners to reveal substats. Stop farming when you have CRIT Rate + CRIT DMG on Cost 4, and at least one CRIT stat on Cost 3/1 pieces. Read Echo Guide →

❓ Common Questions

Who should use Tempest Mephis?

Tempest Mephis is best for any Aero DPS character, providing the Sierra Gale set bonus.

Where do I farm Tempest Mephis?

Drops from Aero Tacet Discords in the overworld. Cost 4 echoes also appear in Tacet Fields. Use the Interactive Map → for spawn locations.

Is Tempest Mephis worth farming?

Yes — as a Cost 4 boss echo with a powerful active skill, this is a priority farm for Sierra Gale set users.
