Lampylumen Myriad

Cost 4 ❄️ Glacio

A shimmering echo composed of countless frozen lights. Each light is a crystallized memory, and together they create a blinding spectacle of icy destruction.

❄️ Glacio
Element
❄️ Freezing Frost
Sonata Effect

Active Skill

Myriad Ice
Summon multiple ice crystals that explode after a delay, dealing Glacio DMG. Each crystal that hits reduces the cooldown of this skill by 1s.

Sonata Effect: ❄️ Freezing Frost

2-piece: Glacio DMG +10%.
5-piece: After casting Basic Attack or Heavy Attack, Glacio DMG +10%. Upon dealing Glacio DMG to Frozen or Slowed enemies, increases CRIT Rate by 20% for 5s.
