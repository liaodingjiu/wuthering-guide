Dreamless

Cost 4 💀 Havoc

An echo born from restless nightmares. It never truly sleeps, and neither do those caught in its wave of endless darkness.

💀 Havoc
Element
💀 Sun-sinking Eclipse
Sonata Effect

Active Skill

Nightmare Wave
Transform into Dreamless and release a wave of Havoc energy, dealing DMG and reducing enemy DEF by 10% for 10s.

Sonata Effect: 💀 Sun-sinking Eclipse

2-piece: Havoc DMG +10%.
5-piece: After casting Basic Attack or Heavy Attack, Havoc DMG +7.5%. Stacks up to 4 times, each lasts 15s.

🗺️ How to Farm

🎯
Target: Tacet Field — Havoc Region

Drops from Havoc Tacet Discords. Cost 4 echoes have higher rates from boss-level enemies. Use Interactive Map → for spawn locations.

🔄
Merge & Upgrade Strategy

Feed unwanted lv.0 echoes to level promising ones to lv.5-10. If substats are bad, feed that echo to the next one — exp transfers efficiently.

💡
Substat Priority: CRIT > ATK% > Flat ATK

Use Tuners to reveal substats. Stop farming when you have CRIT Rate + CRIT DMG on Cost 4, and at least one CRIT stat on Cost 3/1 pieces. Read Echo Guide →

❓ Common Questions

Who should use Dreamless?

Dreamless is best for any Havoc DPS character, providing the Sun-sinking Eclipse set bonus.

Where do I farm Dreamless?

Drops from Havoc Tacet Discords in the overworld. Cost 4 echoes also appear in Tacet Fields. Use the Interactive Map → for spawn locations.

Is Dreamless worth farming?

Yes — as a Cost 4 boss echo with a powerful active skill, this is a priority farm for Sun-sinking Eclipse set users.
