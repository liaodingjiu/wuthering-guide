Stonewall Bracer

Cost 3 ❄️ Glacio

An echo that has perfected the art of defense. Its walls have stood for millennia, and its shattering is as dangerous as any weapon.

❄️ Glacio
Element
❄️ Freezing Frost
Sonata Effect

Active Skill

Fortified Wall
Raise a stone wall that blocks enemy projectiles. When destroyed, deals Glacio DMG to nearby enemies.

Sonata Effect: ❄️ Freezing Frost

2-piece: Glacio DMG +10%.
5-piece: After casting Basic Attack or Heavy Attack, Glacio DMG +10%. Upon dealing Glacio DMG to Frozen or Slowed enemies, increases CRIT Rate by 20% for 5s.

🗺️ How to Farm

🎯
Target: Tacet Field — Glacio Region

Drops from Glacio Tacet Discords. Cost 3 echoes have higher rates from boss-level enemies. Use Interactive Map → for spawn locations.

🔄
Merge & Upgrade Strategy

Feed unwanted lv.0 echoes to level promising ones to lv.5-10. If substats are bad, feed that echo to the next one — exp transfers efficiently.

💡
Substat Priority: CRIT > ATK% > Flat ATK

Use Tuners to reveal substats. Stop farming when you have CRIT Rate + CRIT DMG on Cost 4, and at least one CRIT stat on Cost 3/1 pieces. Read Echo Guide →

❓ Common Questions

Who should use Stonewall Bracer?

Stonewall Bracer is best for any Glacio DPS character, providing the Freezing Frost set bonus.

Where do I farm Stonewall Bracer?

Drops from Glacio Tacet Discords in the overworld. Cost 3 echoes also appear in Tacet Fields. Use the Interactive Map → for spawn locations.

Is Stonewall Bracer worth farming?

Yes — as a Cost 3 piece, farm until you get CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG substats to complete your Freezing Frost set.
