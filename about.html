How We Stay Accurate

Wuthering Waves releases new versions every ~6 weeks. Each patch introduces new characters, weapons, echoes, and balance changes. We:

  • Update within 48 hours of a new patch dropping
  • Cross-reference data across multiple sources before publishing
  • Accept community corrections via email — if you spot an error, let us know
  • Mark all pages with visible update dates so readers know the information is current

Why "WutZone"?

"Wu" from Wuthering Waves, "tZone" from the concept of a trusted zone of information. Short, memorable, and focused — one place for everything Wuthering Waves.
