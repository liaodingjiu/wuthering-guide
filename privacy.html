Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Wuthering Waves Database (wutzone.com) is an independent fan project. We are committed to transparency about how this site operates and what data is involved when you visit.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) from visitors. There is no user registration, no comment system, and no database storing visitor data.

Analytics

We use Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors interact with the site. Clarity provides anonymized heatmaps, session recordings, and usage metrics. It does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or IP addresses in a personally identifiable form.

Clarity may set first-party cookies in your browser for session tracking. These cookies do not identify you personally. You can learn more at clarity.microsoft.com.

Cookies

Our site itself does not set any cookies. Third-party services (Microsoft Clarity, and potentially future advertising partners) may set cookies as described in their respective privacy policies. You can control cookie behavior through your browser settings.

Advertising

We may display advertisements through Google AdSense or similar platforms in the future. These platforms may use cookies to serve relevant ads based on your browsing history. If and when advertising is enabled:

  • A notice will be displayed on the site
  • You may opt out of personalized ads through Google's Ad Settings
  • This privacy policy will be updated accordingly

Children's Privacy
