Version 3.0 — "New Dawn" OFFICIAL

📅 July 3, 2026

Wuthering Waves 3.0 is live. This is a landmark patch introducing a new region, new Resonators, and major system overhauls:

  • New 5★ Character: Chisa — Havoc Broadblade Main DPS. Drains HP for massive AoE burst damage. Signature weapon: Ages of Harvest.
  • New Map Region: "Aurora Heights" — a vertical, multi-layered zone with new Echoes, puzzles, and world bosses.
  • System Update: Echo loadout presets (up to 10 per character), auto-dismantle rules for echoes, and a redesigned Forte upgrade UI.
  • Battle Pass: New BP weapon series with Crit Rate main stats — competitive with standard 5★ options.
  • Free Pulls: 20 free limited pulls via login event + 1,600 Astrite from exploration and events.
