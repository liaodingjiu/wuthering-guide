How Tiers Are Determined

S-Tier — Best-in-class. Dominant performance in their role. Optimal in virtually all content.
A-Tier — Excellent. Strong performers that compete closely with S-Tier in the right team.
B-Tier — Solid. Viable choices with clear strengths but require more investment or specific teams.
C-Tier — Niche. Usable but outclassed in their role by higher-tier options.
D-Tier — For completionists. Not recommended for resource investment.

Rankings are based on verified stats, practical testing, and community consensus. Data sourced from wutheringwaves.fandom.com.
